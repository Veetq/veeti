Your data is stored temporarily for analytics purposes and is deleted after a certain period. We do not store personal data for longer than necessary.

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.
